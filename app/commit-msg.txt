fix: gate duties in planning mode by canEditDuties

Duties in planning mode were shown and assignable using the meeting
schedule right (canEditMidweekSchedule/canEditWeekendSchedule) rather
than the dedicated canEditDuties right, so a non-admin with schedule
access but without duties access saw and could touch duties. PlanningMode
now takes canEditDuties; when false, zoneDuties is empty, so the Duties
group is hidden, excluded from the progress count, and the picker is
unreachable. Matches how DutiesSection is gated on the main screen.
