fix: save invited (out-of-town) speaker immediately

The invited-speaker fields (speakerName, speakerCongregation) only
called update() on local form state, never onInstantSave — like the
public-talk pick before. In the bottom sheet (no submit button) the
invited speaker was lost on close and the row showed "unassigned". The
fields now queueInstant their value, and the local/invited toggle
instant-saves the cleared opposite fields. Mirrors the publisher picker
which already saved instantly.
