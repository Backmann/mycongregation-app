fix: planning-mode rows now open the editor

Tapping a row in planning mode did nothing: PlanningMode (a Modal)
called the outer onEdit→setEditing, but AssignmentSheet (also a Modal)
was mounted as a sibling beneath the planning Modal and stayed hidden
behind it. PlanningMode now owns its own editing state and renders the
AssignmentSheet inside its own Modal, so tapping a part opens the editor
over the planning screen as intended.
