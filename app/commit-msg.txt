fix: planning mode reflects assignments as you make them

PlanningMode received a frozen snapshot of items captured when the zone
was opened, so assigning someone in the sheet never updated the list or
progress. It now subscribes to the same ['assignments', weekStartISO]
query and derives its items by eventType (weekend minus hospitality), so
saving an assignment immediately moves the row out of "needs assigning"
and advances the progress bar. The planningZone state type carries
weekStartISO/nextWeekISO instead of an items snapshot.
