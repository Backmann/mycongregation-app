fix: add a smart back button to the My Tasks screen

Headers are globally disabled (headerShown: false), and the My Tasks
screen had no header of its own, so opening it from Home left no way
back. Added a header with a back arrow that goes back through history
when possible and falls back to Home otherwise
(router.canGoBack() ? back() : replace('/home')), so the screen is
always exitable regardless of how it was reached.
