feat: redesign the schedule sections

Refreshes the week view into clean, card-style collapsible sections with
a visual language borrowed from the printed meeting workbook. Each
section now carries its own accent colour and a tinted icon: midweek and
weekend meetings, duties (teal), field-service (green) and cleaning
(cyan). CollapsibleMeetingBlock gains accent and icon props, an animated
open/close (LayoutAnimation), a rounded card with soft elevation and a
colour-coded progress badge (green when complete, amber when gaps
remain). Inside duties, each day is marked with a coloured chip instead
of faint grey text, and assignee names read in stronger contrast so the
section no longer looks disabled.
