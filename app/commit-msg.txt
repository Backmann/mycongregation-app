feat: duties in planning mode

Planning mode now also covers the meeting's duties (microphones, stage,
audio, security, etc.), not just the programme. It subscribes to the
duties query for the zone's week, filters by eventType, and shows the
unassigned ones in a "Duties" group that counts toward the zone's
progress. Tapping a duty opens a compact publisher picker (filtered by
the duty's capability); choosing someone assigns it and refreshes the
list. Cleaning slots are a separate follow-up.
