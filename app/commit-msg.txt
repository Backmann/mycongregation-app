fix: show clean prayer name in My Tasks

The opening/closing prayer tasks displayed their imported partTitle,
which often joins an adjacent part or song with " | " (e.g. "Песня 5 и
молитва | Вступительные слова"). That extra context confuses the person
assigned, who only needs to know it's the opening or closing prayer.
Prayers now show just the translated part name via getPartLabel, like
the schedule. Other parts are unchanged.
