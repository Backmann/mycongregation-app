feat: role icons in the duties picker

Each duty in the duties section now shows a tinted icon in a rounded
circle to the left of its picker — shield for security, mic for
microphone, video camera for video, screen for stage, and so on — each
with its own accent colour. PublisherSelector gains optional roleIcon
and roleColor props and lays the trigger out horizontally when they are
set; without them it renders exactly as before, so its other uses
(meeting assignments) are unchanged. This completes the schedule
redesign so the duties section matches the approved mockup.
