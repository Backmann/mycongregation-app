feat: collapsible sections in the schedule

The schedule is now five independent collapsible sections, in order:
midweek meeting, weekend meeting, duties, field-service meetings and
cleaning. Each expands and collapses on its own and starts collapsed,
so the week is compact and you open what you need.

Duties are no longer nested at the bottom of each meeting; they get
their own section holding both the midweek and weekend duty groups,
each with its targeted plan button. Field-service meetings and cleaning
are wrapped in the same collapsible container as the meetings, and their
own inline headers are hidden (via a new hideHeader prop) to avoid a
double title. CollapsibleMeetingBlock gains an optional showBadge so the
non-meeting sections render without a progress badge.

Replaces the earlier tab experiment, which mixed unrelated sections
under one tab.
