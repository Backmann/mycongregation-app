fix: sort My Tasks by program order within a day

Tasks on the same day were shown in arrival order, so a meeting's parts
could appear out of sequence (e.g. closing words before the chairman).
They now sort by partOrder as a secondary key after date, matching the
program (chairman = 1 ... closing prayer = last). Non-meeting tasks
(duties, cleaning) have no partOrder and fall to the end of the day.
Requires the server partOrder change.
