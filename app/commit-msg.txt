feat: one publish dialog everywhere (notify / silent)

Publishing now goes through a single PublishDialog from every entry
point — the publish buttons on the midweek/weekend meeting blocks and
the one in planning mode all open the same notify/silent/cancel dialog.
Previously the block buttons always notified (no choice) and only
planning mode offered silent, so behaviour was inconsistent. Planning
mode drops its two-button bar for one button that opens the dialog.
