fix: label each day inside the duties section

In the new collapsible duties section the midweek and weekend duty
groups were stacked with no indication which was which (hideHeader had
removed the per-group label along with the section title). Each group
now shows its day — "Встреча в будний день" / "Встреча в выходной день"
— via getEventTypeLabel, matching the meeting section headers. The label
is shown in the only/embedded mode too, not just the standalone list.
