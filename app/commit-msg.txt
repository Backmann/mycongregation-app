fix: sheet form shows current values for the opened assignment

AssignmentForm reads initial only on mount (useState), but the Modal in
the sheet reuses its subtree, so opening an already-filled part showed
empty/stale fields — you had to re-enter both talk and speaker even to
change one. The form now carries key={assignment.id}: switching parts
remounts it with the live values (talk, speaker, assistant, status,
notes), while editing the same part keeps your input. Changing only the
talk or only the speaker now leaves the other intact.
