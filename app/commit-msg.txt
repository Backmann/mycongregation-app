feat: self-service absences in the app UI

Wires up the client side of self-service absences (server side already
authorizes own-absence writes). The Absences tile is now shown to
everyone, not just managers. A regular publisher sees only their own
absences and files new ones with their publisher fixed (the picker is
hidden and the directory is not queried, which they can't access);
managers keep the full picker and see everyone. Adds an "add absence"
button to the list and an absences.add label in all locales.
