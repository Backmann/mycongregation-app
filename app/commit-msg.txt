fix: localize weekend part titles in my-assignments

Weekend assignment rows showed raw part keys (weekend_chairman,
weekend_opening_prayer) because taskTitle used item.label verbatim, and
the aggregator puts the partKey there when a part has no human title
(unlike EPUB-imported parts). taskTitle now runs the label through
getPartLabel: keys in the registry are translated ("Председатель
встречи"), human titles pass through unchanged. Fixes both the Home
feed and the My-assignments screen for everyone.
